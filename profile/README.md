 EarTrumpet App

**EarTrumpet App** is a powerful, flexible audio management solution designed to help users control volume per application, route sound devices, and fix audio chaos.  
With **EarTrumpet App**, you can replace the default volume mixer with a modern, clickable interface.  
**EarTrumpet App** supports per-app volume, system tray integration, keyboard shortcuts, and dark theme, making it an essential tool for streamers, developers, designers, and everyday users.

## About EarTrumpet App

**EarTrumpet App** brings back sanity to audio control. Unlike the hidden system mixer, **EarTrumpet App** lives in your taskbar and gives you one-click access to every app that plays sound.  
**EarTrumpet App** works with any audio device (headphones, speakers, HDMI, USB) and provides real-time volume adjustments without opening system panels.  
Whether you need to mute a noisy browser, boost a game, or balance a conference call, **EarTrumpet App** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**EarTrumpet App** respects your privacy — no data collection, no cloud tracking, just local audio control.  
With **EarTrumpet App**, you gain clarity, reduce distraction, and control every sound source instantly.
